Nagarkot

Famous for its stunning sunrise and sunset views over the Himalayas, Nagarkot is a popular hill station offering a serene escape from city life. The area is also known for its hiking trails and lush landscapes.

Kathmandu Durbar Square

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Kathmandu Durbar Square is a historical marvel filled with ancient palaces, temples, and vibrant culture, showcasing the rich history of the Kathmandu Valley. It's a bustling hub for locals and tourists alike.

Chandragiri Hills

Offering panoramic views of the Kathmandu Valley and the Himalayas, Chandragiri Hills is accessible by cable car, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The area features hiking trails and lush greenery.

Langtang National Park

Renowned for its breathtaking landscapes and rich biodiversity, Langtang National Park is a haven for trekkers and nature enthusiasts. It boasts stunning mountain views, diverse wildlife, and vibrant local culture.

Bhaktapur Durbar Square

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Bhaktapur Durbar Square is a stunning showcase of medieval architecture, featuring intricate temples, palaces, and vibrant local culture. Known for its well-preserved buildings and lively atmosphere, it offers a glimpse into the rich history and artistry of the Kathmandu Valley.

Pashupatinath Temple

One of the holiest Hindu temples in Nepal, Pashupatinath Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and attracts pilgrims from around the world. Its stunning architecture and sacred ceremonies along the Bagmati River make it a significant cultural site.

Boudhanath Stupa

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Boudhanath Stupa is one of the largest stupas in Nepal and a major pilgrimage site for Buddhists. Its striking architecture and vibrant surrounding monasteries create a peaceful and spiritual atmosphere.
